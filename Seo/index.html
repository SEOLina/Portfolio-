About me

My copywriting career started in 2020 on freelance platforms, where I took on simple projects for low rates. However, I’ve always been more interested in offering solutions that truly help my clients rather than just completing a task. I soon noticed that SEO copywriting was in higher demand, so I deepened my expertise in this area. I learned how to not just insert keywords, but to analyze content for effective search engine optimization.

Realizing that I couldn’t fully develop my skills or grow beyond a certain point on freelance platforms, I shifted focus to conducting SEO audits, identifying errors, and providing optimization recommendations. My approach has always been about solving real problems for clients, not just fulfilling an order.

Since 2022, I’ve expanded my knowledge into social media marketing, recognizing that successful copywriting today requires building full strategies. I began creating sales funnels, content plans, and helping experts with their online positioning. My goal has always been to create high-quality content that directly addresses the needs of the audience and contributes to business growth.
